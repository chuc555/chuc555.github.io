"Using your knowledge of Javascript so far, design and develop a single-serve website that utilizes cookies. Using at least one non-standard input (i.e. other than a mouse click), this website should exhibit a narrative loop in which the end-user feels like they are progressing despite engaging in a (endlessly looping) pattern of action."

1. Growth: A cycle of growing your own plant. (Interaction: click)

2. Stack: Make an endless stack; could be anything but in this case I used paper to illustrate the motion. (Interaction: keyup or keydown)

3. Day + Night: Cycle through time as days and nights pass. (Interaction: scroll maybe?)

4. Inflate: Inflate a balloon and watch it float away. Repeat. (Interaction: keyup or keydown)
